Here is one of the songs I am most proud of, as it is one of my few attempts at making electronic music. I was inspired to make the beat one rainy summer afternoon in 2017 while I was laying in bed listening to my air conditioner. There was a previous version of the beat where the main synthesizer was made with Serum, the wavetable editing program, however I've since lost access to that program and so this is a close approximation of what it originally sounded like. The original synth made with Serum would've sounded much more metallic and a little "sharper".
